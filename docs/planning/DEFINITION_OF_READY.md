# Definition Of Ready

Related: `docs/planning/STORIES_TEMPLATE.md`, `docs/product/ACCEPTANCE_CRITERIA.md`

A story is ready when all are true:

- Problem statement is clear and scoped.
- Acceptance criteria are testable and unambiguous.
- Domain context and bounded context are identified.
- API/event/schema impacts are documented.
- Telemetry expectations are specified.
- Risks and dependencies are listed.
- Rollback intent is defined.
