# Ownership and Support

## Purpose
Define ownership defaults and support expectations for this repository stage.

## Audience
- Engineers and operators assigning responsibility.
- AI agents documenting ownership boundaries.

## Scope
Applies to planning and operational ownership documentation.

## Ownership Defaults
- Product owner: founding team.
- Engineering owner: founding team.
- Operations owner: engineering owner.
- Catalog quality owner: product owner with operator support.

## Decision Authority
- Architecture decisions must be captured in ADRs.
- Product scope decisions must be captured in product/planning docs.
- External contract changes must be reviewed by engineering ownership.

## Support Posture
- Support should prioritize incident severity.
- Incident notes should include trigger, impact, and resolution.
