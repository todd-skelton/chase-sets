# Glossary

## Purpose
Provide a compact alias of key terms for agent prompts.

## Canonical Terms
- `Identity`: authentication subject.
- `Account`: profile linked to an identity.
- `Organization`: seller team boundary.
- `Listing`: seller intent to sell.
- `Offer`: buyer intent to buy.
- `Order`: seller-scoped commitment.
- `Shipment`: fulfillment unit.
- `Payout`: transfer of settled funds.
- `Dispute`: post-payment issue flow.
- `Projection`: read model derived from events.
