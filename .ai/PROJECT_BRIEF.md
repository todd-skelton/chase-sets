# Project Brief

## Purpose
Summarize product intent and constraints for planning and architecture work.

## Audience
- AI agents drafting design and requirements updates.
- Engineers preparing implementation plans.

## Problem
Collectors and sellers need a predictable marketplace with clear catalog semantics, reliable transactions, and transparent outcomes.

## Core Flows
1. Identity and account setup.
2. Listing and discovery.
3. Offer and match.
4. Checkout and payment.
5. Fulfillment and delivery.
6. Payout and dispute handling.

## Constraints
- Event sourcing is a core architectural commitment.
- Stripe is the baseline payment provider.
- Deployment posture is container-first and cloud-agnostic.
